Devoted Civcraft Server

A political Minecraft server running Civcraft plugins.

What plugins are you running?

We have all the plugins you'd expect for a civilization server, Citadel, PrisonPearl, JukeAlert, and other custom built plugins to balance the map and allow for real political interaction.

How are you different from HCF's CivCraft?

We are a more community based game that relies on people and minimal game interaction. What you get is more of a roleplaying feel, and less of a board game.

How are you different from the original Civcraft?

If you check out the Civcraft wiki you'll notice that with each version their 'tech tree' is getting more complicated, grindy, and not even necessarily longer. We focus on making our server as easily as possible to understand and play on, to allow for more players to participate.
