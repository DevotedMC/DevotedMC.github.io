Devoted - JukeAlert Help and Download

We are a nation building server that uses JukeAlert to protect player items against grief.

How does JukeAlert work?

Simple reinforce a noteblock or a jukebox with a citadel reinforcement to create a snitch. This snitch will record all actions that occur in an 11 block cube from the snitch itself. Use /jainfo to check what players did in the snitched area while you were gone.
