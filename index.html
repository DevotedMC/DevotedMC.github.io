About Us

Devoted is a civilization server. The goal is simple, see what happens in a total sandbox. We have nations, civilization mods/plugins, and political intrigue. Some see if your nation can last through war, at our server ip: play.devotedmc.com

So what is so different about Devoted Minecraft?

We have a variety of server-side mods to facilitate strong, stable groups of players to unify and develop communities, much like starting nations.

Participation

At the end of the day, at our core, we're a civilization minecraft server, an experiment in what makes nations work.

Benefits

If you join our server, you can start building a civilization. Come join our immersive world and the community that surrounds it. We're a lot like other servers like civilizationcraft, civcraft, and others. We've been described as the Minecraft Eve Online server.
